Thinking Style: Concrete Random

The description I found when googling around for "Concrete Random" was this:

30 % of the General Population falls into this category. You want to know what the point is to all this anyway. You are a great trouble-shooter and experimenter.You are the practical dreamer. You are very perceptive. You have lots of projects going and have trouble finishing any of them. Best Career Choices: Finance, Computers and Mental Health.

Reading some of the assertions about Concrete Random thinkers, the parallels to myself are extremely close. I find it difficult to put stock into these personality identifier quizzes in general, but this one really hit home. I think the best part was this,"Your validation process is done via practical demonstration and personal proof. You rarely accept outside authority." Not only has this been the case for me my entire life (especially in a school setting) but I think it has a huge bearing on how I am learning to code. It's not good enough for me see something that works and imitate it, I have to know the WHY, which 9 times out of 10 makes my learning process much, much longer. Once I understand the why of something, my ability to retain it increases dramatically. I think for me personally, especially during Phase 0 I'm not going to have all of my questions answered in a satisfactory way. I think a lot of getting through this Phase 0 is accepting the fact that I wont have answers to everything, and have to keep muscling along.
